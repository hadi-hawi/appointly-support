Appointly – Support

If you need help using Appointly or have any questions, please contact our support team.

Contact Support

  • Email: support@appointly.app
  • Response time: within 24–48 hours

Before contacting support, please include:

  • Your device model and operating system
  • App version
  • A clear description of the issue
  • Screenshots or screen recordings (if available)

For information about how we handle your data, please review our Privacy Policy.
